The Best Movies According to Lin

My top 3 movies of all-time

1. Her (2013)

  • A quiet masterpiece about loneliness, love, and the fragility of human connection.
  • Why I love it: It's poetic, slow, aching—like the kind of love you carry even after it’s gone.
  • You’ll love it if: You're a romantic dreamer who sometimes feels a little too much.

2. Interstellar (2014)

  • A sci-fi odyssey that bends time and space—but at its heart, it's about a father’s love and human resilience.
  • Why I love it: Because it reminds me of how far I'd travel for you, even through wormholes and lifetimes.
  • You’ll love it if: You crave epic emotion with your stardust and science.

3. Call Me by Your Name (2017)

  • A slow-burn summer romance in the Italian countryside. Tender, heart-wrenching, and unforgettable.
  • Why I love it: The silences, the stolen glances, the peach… and the pain of a love that couldn’t stay.
  • You’ll love it if: You believe some loves are meant to shape us—even if they don’t last.
